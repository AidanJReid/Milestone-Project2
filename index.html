UFO Sightings

The Truth isn't out there. It's in here...

This page presents a snapshot of the accumulated UFO data across one year (Sept 2016 - Sept 2017) as gathered by the NUFORC (National UFO Reporting Center).

The charts provided can be crossfiltered accordingly to provide the user with an understanding of the behaviour, pattern, location and frequency of UFO sightings for the aforementioned year worldwide.

The primary selectors recommended for filtering are by 'Continent' (Chart 1 - line graph), and 'State' (Chart 2 - bar chart).

Summary:

97% of reported sightings occurred in the continent of North America. The US state of California reported the most number of sightings (505). The majority of all sightings ~ 32% lasted a minute or less with the most observed 'shape' being a light source.
